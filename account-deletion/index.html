Delete My Account & Data

You can delete your FarmFlow account and the personal data associated with it at any time — from the app, or right here without the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

Open FarmFlow and go to Settings → Account → Delete account. You'll be asked to confirm. Deletion begins immediately.

Option 2 — From this page (no app needed)

Email us from the address on your FarmFlow account:

Request deletion by email

Use the subject line "Account deletion request". We'll verify the request came from your account's email address and confirm once deletion is complete. Requests are actioned promptly.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile, including display name and town
  • All of your listings and listing photos
  • Your messages and conversations
  • Your saved favourites and preferences
  • Your marketing consent record (your consent is withdrawn)

What we keep, and why

A small amount of data is retained after deletion where the law requires it or where it's needed to protect other users:

DataRetentionReason
Purchase and payment ledger records (donations, boosts, verification fees)For as long as accounting and tax law requiresAccounting and tax law
Records of enforcement actions (bans, fraud reports)For as long as needed to protect the platform and its usersPreventing banned users from returning and protecting other users
Verification documents (Verified Sellers only)For as long as regulatory obligations requireRegulatory record-keeping obligations

Retained records are kept securely, are not used for any other purpose, and are deleted when the retention period ends.

Before you go

  • Deletion is permanent — your listings, messages, and verified status (if any) cannot be recovered.
  • If you only want to stop marketing emails, you don't need to delete your account: turn them off in Settings → Notifications or use the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • If something's wrong with the app, tell us — we may be able to fix it.
