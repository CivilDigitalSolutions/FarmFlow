Prohibited Items Policy

FarmFlow is for lawful agricultural trade. Here's what can't be listed — and what needs verification first.

Effective date: 7 July 2026 · This policy forms part of the Terms & Conditions

Never allowed

Listings in these categories are removed and may lead to account suspension:

  • Illegal drugs, drug paraphernalia, and controlled substances of any kind
  • Weapons, ammunition, and explosives
  • Endangered or protected species, and any part or product of them (CITES-listed plants and animals)
  • Stolen goods, or goods you are not authorised to sell
  • Counterfeit products, including fake branded agrochemicals, seeds, or equipment
  • Restricted agrochemicals and pesticides banned or unregistered in Jamaica or Trinidad & Tobago
  • Human or animal medicines requiring a prescription or veterinary authorisation
  • Alcohol and tobacco products
  • Anything unrelated to agriculture used to disguise a prohibited sale
  • Services or goods that violate any applicable law

Restricted — Verified Sellers only

These categories are legal to trade but regulated. They may only be listed by sellers approved under the Verified Seller Programme for that specific category:

CategoryWhy it's restricted
Agricultural machinery & equipmentProof of ownership required to combat equipment theft (serial numbers, bills of sale)
Live animals & livestockAnimal traceability and welfare rules (e.g. NAITS cattle traceability in Jamaica; movement permits)
Meat & animal productsFood safety and public health law (butcher's licensing, veterinary inspection)

Attempting to list restricted items without verified status will result in removal. Repeated attempts may lead to suspension.

Listing standards

Even for allowed items, listings must use real photos of the actual goods, honest descriptions and quantities, genuine asking prices, and the true location of the goods. Duplicate listings and keyword spam are removed.

Something look wrong?

If you see a listing that breaks these rules, report it in the app — every listing has a report option — or contact support. Reports are confidential.
