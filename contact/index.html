Before you write in

  • Report in the app first for problem listings or users — reports reach moderators fastest through the report button on the listing or chat.
  • Check the FAQs — most common questions are answered there.
  • Account deletion has its own dedicated process: Delete My Account & Data.

What to include

  • The email address on your FarmFlow account
  • Your island and town (helps with location issues)
  • A screenshot, if the problem is visual
  • The listing title or link, if your question is about a listing
